Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs with paprika.

Ingredients

  • 6 Large Eggs
  • 3 Tbsp. Mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp. Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp. Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • Paprika, for garnish

Steps

  1. Hard boil eggs, then peel and slice in half lengthwise.
  2. Spoon the egg yolks into a separate bowl.
  3. Mash the yolks and add the mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, salt and pepper. Stir until smooth.
  4. Use the spoon to add a portion of the deviled egg mixture back into each egg white half.
  5. Sprinkle paprika for garnish
