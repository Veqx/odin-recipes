Spaghetti

Spaghetti and meatballs with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 3 Tbsp. Basil
  • 3 Tbsp. Parsley
  • 1 tsp. Oregano
  • 3/4 tsp. Salt
  • 1/4 tsp. Black Pepper
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 1-1/2 lbs. ground meat mixture
  • 3/4 Cup Dried Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 Cup Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 32 oz. Marinara Sauce
  • 1 lb. Spaghetti Noodles

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the egg, basil, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper, garlic and water; whisk well.
  2. Add the meat, breadcrumbs and Parmigiano Reggiano. Mix with your hands until just combined.
  3. Roll the mixture into golf ball-sized meatballs, and place on an ungreased baking sheet.
  4. Bake for about 10 minutes, then remove the baking sheet from the oven and use a metal spatula or tongs to turn the meatballs.
  5. Put the meatballs back in the oven and cook for another 10 minutes, until they are nicely browned and almost cooked through.
  6. Bring the marinara sauce to a simmer in a large skillet. Season to taste.
  7. Transfer the browned meatballs to the marinara sauce, leaving the fat behind.
  8. Cover loosely with a lid or foil and simmer for about 10 minutes, until the meatballs are cooked through. Keep warm until ready to toss with pasta.
  9. Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil.
  10. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente. Drain, then toss with the sauce and meatballs.
  11. Transfer the spaghetti and meatballs to serving bowls and top with fresh chopped basil and more grated cheese.
